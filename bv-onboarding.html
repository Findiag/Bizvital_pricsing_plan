Phase 1 — HookDay 1–3 · Get them to their first result fast
D1
Welcome + instant win Aha moment
User signs up. Redirect to a 3-step setup wizard: connect accounting data, pick industry, set company size. Goal: get their first Financial Diagnostic score on screen within 10 minutes. Show Growth (1.1) and Profitability (1.2) scores visually. Also unlock Sales (2.1) and Finance (2.2) department views immediately — show how both departments are performing side by side.
Email: "Your financial health score is ready — take a look" · Show scores with one CTA: "See your full report"
D2
Efficiency + department teaser Engagement
Show Efficiency module (1.3) result with a plain-language explanation. Alongside it, show a locked preview of the Management (2.3) and Operations (2.5) department views with a "4 more departments waiting" prompt. Goal: they see their Sales and Finance dept health, but realise there's a full company picture they're missing.
Email: "63/100 on Efficiency — and your Sales dept has a flag we need to show you" · Reference their Sales dept score from 2.1 to make it personal.
D3
Locked modules teaser Upgrade nudge
Show blurred previews of Business Risk (1.5), Liquidity (1.7), and the 4 locked departments (Management, Material, Operations, HR) all on one screen with lock icons. Tooltip: "Upgrade to Growth to unlock your complete business health picture — all 8 financial modules and all 6 departments."
Email: "We found something in your Liquidity score — and 4 of your departments are waiting to be checked" · Two hooks in one. One CTA: "Unlock for ₹999/mo"
Phase 2 — ValueDay 4–7 · Show what the product can really do
D4
Report preview — shareable moment Aha moment
Unlock Report Preview (feature 8) fully. Show a beautiful 1-page financial health summary including their Sales and Finance department scores. Add a "Share with your CA" button (disabled with upgrade tooltip). They want to share this — and they need to upgrade to download it.
Email: "Your 1-page financial health report is ready to share" · Screenshot of report. "Download PDF — upgrade to Growth."
D5
AI observations teaser Upgrade nudge
Show a blurred AI observation card with 2 visible lines specific to their data: "Your gross margin has declined 4.2%..." then fade. This is feature 10 — your biggest upgrade driver. The visible lines must feel genuinely specific, not generic.
Email: "Your AI CFO has 3 observations — including one about your Operations department" · Mention a locked department to deepen curiosity.
D6
Social proof + industry case study Engagement
"A textile trader in Surat found ₹2.3L/month in profit leakage AND discovered their HR department had the highest cost-efficiency gap across the business — something they'd never seen in a single report before." Mention the cross-functional diagnostic by name. Story format always outperforms feature lists.
Email: "How a Surat trader found ₹2.3L and a hidden HR problem in the same week"
D7
Profit Leakage + full department reveal Biggest aha Conversion trigger
Show a "potential leakage detected" banner with a ₹ range AND a teaser of what all 6 department diagnostics would reveal. "Your Sales dept score is 68 — but your Operations and Material depts are unreviewed. Want to see if a department is pulling your profits down?" Lock the full breakdown behind Growth. This is your single most powerful conversion trigger.
Email: "We found potential profit leakage — and 4 of your departments haven't been checked yet" · Big number + department mystery = irresistible.
Phase 3 — ConvertDay 8–11 · Remove every reason not to upgrade
D8
Upgrade offer — first conversion window Conversion window
Limited-time offer: "Upgrade today and get 2 extra months free on annual billing." Show the Growth plan features side by side with what they currently see — make the gap between 2 departments and 6 departments feel obvious. The department gap is your most visual upgrade argument.
Email: "Trial day 8 — unlock all 6 departments + everything else, 2 months free today" · Annual pricing front and centre. 48-hour countdown.
D9
Human touch — founder call offer High-intent close
For first 50 customers: personalised message from the founder. "Hi [Name], I noticed you've been checking your Sales and Finance departments — would love to show you what the full 6-department picture reveals for a business like [company name]. 20 minutes?" Use this aggressively at MVP stage.
Email: "[First name], can we show you the full picture for [Company]?" · Personal, short. Calendly link. Signed by founder.
D10
Usage summary + peer benchmarking Re-engagement
Send activity summary: departments viewed, financial health score vs industry average. "Businesses in your industry average 71/100 on Liquidity — you're at 58. And they're checking 6 departments — you're only seeing 2." Benchmarking against peers is one of the strongest B2B SaaS triggers.
Email: "You're seeing 2 of 6 departments — here's what your competitors are checking" · Benchmarking + department gap.
D11
Voice Note AI teaser — the surprise Novelty aha
Play a 30-second sample AI voice note covering both financial health AND a department flag. "This week, your Sales department health dropped from 72 to 64 — here's why and what to do." Feature 12 is your most novel feature. Let them hear it before they buy it.
Email: "Listen: what your AI CFO would say about your Sales department this week" · Link to 30s audio clip.
Phase 4 — Close or NurtureDay 12–14 · Last call without being annoying
D12
3 days left reminder Urgency
Simple, honest nudge. Show exactly what locks: all 6 financial modules beyond 1.3, 4 department diagnostics, all analysis tools, AI features. Include a "not ready?" link to extend trial by 7 days in exchange for a 10-minute feedback call — user research + pipeline warmth in one.
Email: "3 days left — your Sales dept, Liquidity score, and Profit Leakage scan will lock" · Specific features, not vague.
D13
Last best offer Final push
Annual Growth plan + 1 free month of Enterprise features (CA access, team collab). Make it feel exclusive — "only for trial users who haven't upgraded yet." Scarcity + sweetener = highest conversion of all 14 days.
Email: "Last chance — annual Growth + 1 month Enterprise free, just for you" · Expires at trial end.
D14
Trial end — two paths Retain or re-engage
If converted: Welcome email, onboarding call booked, wallet credits activated. Ask for a LinkedIn testimonial or WhatsApp referral — your first 50 customers are your best sales team.

If not converted: Graceful exit. "Your data is safe for 30 days." Add to 30-day drip: one email at day 7, one at day 30 with a new feature update. Do not spam.
Converted: "Welcome to Bizvitals Growth — here's what happens next"  ·  Churned: "Your data is safe. We'll be here when you're ready."
