Bizvitals · Elevator Pitches

25 pitches —
each under 10 seconds

Each pitch communicates one core idea about Bizvitals in under 10 seconds. Use them to open a call, respond to "what do you do?", or break the ice. Pick the one that fits the moment — rotate them across the week so the team doesn't sound scripted.

Rule
One pitch per call. Don't stack them. Pick one, say it cleanly, then pause.
Rotate
Each team member should try at least 5 different pitches and find which ones get the best reactions.
Timing
Practice saying each one in under 10 seconds. Time yourself. If it takes 15 seconds — cut it.
After the pitch
Always follow with a question or pause. "Does that sound relevant to your business?" works every time.
