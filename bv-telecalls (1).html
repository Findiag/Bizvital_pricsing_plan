Bizvitals · Telecall Scripts

5 scripts per person —
built on their elevator pitches

Each team member has 5 complete call scripts, each built around one of their assigned elevator pitches. Rotate scripts daily. Same structure every time: intro → pitch → why it matters → book the demo.

Call goal
Book a 20-min demo call. Not sell. Not explain everything. Just book the demo.
Calling from
Findiag Innovation Pvt. Ltd. — makers of Bizvitals
Duration
30 – 60 seconds max
How to use these scripts
1
Use Script 1 on Monday, Script 2 on Tuesday, and so on. Rotate every day so you never sound the same.
2
Read the pitch 3 times out loud before your first call. Know it well enough to say it in your own voice.
3
Pause after the pitch. Silence is powerful. Let them respond before you say anything else.
4
If they say yes — stop pitching immediately. Book the slot. Don't keep talking.
5
Always offer 2 time slots. "Does Tuesday at 11 or Thursday at 3 work?" is easier to say yes to than "when are you free?"

Is my data secure? What happens to my financial data?
"Completely secure. Your data is encrypted with bank-grade encryption — the same standard your bank uses. It is only used to generate your diagnosis and is never shared, never sold, never stored beyond what's needed. You stay in full control at all times. Our founder personally stands behind this."
Say this calmly and with full confidence. Data security is an emotional concern — don't rush it.
What about the privacy of my company's financial information?
"Your financial data is completely private — only you can see your diagnosis. We don't share it with any third party, we don't retain it after your session, and we don't use it without your consent. Your data belongs to you, always. Happy to share our privacy policy before the demo if that helps."
Offering the privacy policy proactively builds trust — even if they don't ask for it.
I already have a CA / CFO doing this — why do I need Bizvitals?
"Bizvitals isn't here to replace your CA or CFO — it works alongside them. Your CA handles compliance and filings. Your CFO advises on strategy. But neither of them gives you an instant answer at 10pm on a Sunday when you're wondering why your cash is tight. Bizvitals does. And when you do sit with your CA or CFO next time, you'll already know the right questions to ask."
Never make them feel their CA/CFO is being replaced. Always position as complementary.
What does your AI actually do? How does it work?
"Our AI isn't a general chatbot — it's purpose-built specifically for financial diagnosis of Indian businesses. It reads your balance sheet and P&L, applies over 30 financial health checks, benchmarks your numbers against thousands of real Indian companies, and writes a plain-language report — profit leakage in rupees, department health scores, risk flags, and prioritised next steps. Trained on real Indian company data including listed firms — so it understands Indian accounting standards. It doesn't give generic advice — it gives answers specific to your business."
★ They're genuinely curious here — explain with pride. Don't oversell. The specificity does the work.
I can't share my confidential / crucial financial documents
"Completely understand — and we're not asking for anything confidential. All we need is your Balance Sheet and Profit & Loss statement — these are publicly available financial documents that every business already files with their CA or auditor. We don't need your bank statements, GST data, client details, employee records, or any internal business information. Just the two standard documents your CA already has."
★ This objection usually comes from a misunderstanding — they think we need sensitive internal data. Clarify quickly and confidently. If they're still unsure, add: "In fact, for listed companies this data is publicly available on the stock exchange. We just make it useful for private businesses too."
